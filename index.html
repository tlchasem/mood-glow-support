Common Questions

Does Mood Glow require an account?
No — Mood Glow is completely private and requires no login or signup.

Does the app collect data?
No. Mood Glow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Do I need an internet connection?
No — the app works fully offline.

How do I turn the glow on?
Tap the power button on the Home Screen to activate mood lighting. Tap anywhere on the glow screen to return home.

About Mood Glow

Mood Glow was designed to provide a beautiful, minimal, color-based ambience for relaxation, focus, sleep, or meditation. We’re continually improving the app, and we welcome your ideas.
